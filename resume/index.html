§ Coranna Howard

Coranna is a programmer & multi-media crafter from the Seattle, Washington area. She's obsessed with systematic simplicity, writing beautiful code, cats, and the finer details.

Her experience highlights: software management, design exploration, tech consultation, technical writing, vocational teaching, data architecture & reverse-engineering, graphics systems, software repackaging, and the design & implementation of cohesive systems.
